How It Works

QR/NFC ID Cards

Each user carries a unique card that stores essential contact and ID details, making it easy to use.

Smart Booths

Secure telephone booths at key locations read the card and connect the call instantly to a pre-defined contact.

Features

No Mobile Needed

Ideal for children, seniors, or areas with poor mobile access, ensuring everyone stays connected.

Location-Based Access

Booths are strategically placed near schools, markets, and public zones for easy access.

Real-Time Notifications

Family members get an instant alert with the user's location when a call is initiated.

Secure and Private

User data is encrypted and protected with industry-standard security measures to ensure privacy.

About Us

Safe-Connect is founded with a mission to provide secure communication options for individuals without access to smartphones. Our system ensures connectivity, safety, and ease of use for everyone, bridging the digital divide with simple, effective technology.
